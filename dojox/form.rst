dojox.form project
==================

The dojox.form project provides additional form-related widgets beyond `dijit.form </dijit/form>`_ functionality. 

* BusyButton
* CheckedMultiSelect
* DateTextBox
* DropDownSelect
* DropDownStack
* FileInput (base, Auto, and Blind)
* FilePickerTextBox
* `FileUploader <form/FileUploader>`_
* MultiComboBox
* PasswordValidator
* RadioStack
* RangeSliders
* Rating
* TimeSpinner
